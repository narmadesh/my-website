How to Reach Satlok Ashram, Hisar - Tohana Road, Barwala, Hisar, India

By Road

Satlok Ashram is situated 3 kms away from Barwala City on Hisar - Tohana Road.

Distance from Delhi ISBT (Interstate Bus Stand) to Barwala: 191 Kms

Distance from Hisar to Barwala: 30 Kms
Distance from Jind to Barwala: 45 Kms

Delhi to Barwala (Hisar) via Bus

From Delhi ISBT board a bus to Hisar. The journey time is approximately 5 hours. From Hisar take a bus to Barwala. From counter number 1 there is a regular bus service from Hisar to Chandigarh. This bus goes via Barwala. Travel time from Hisar to Barwala is approximately 40 minutes.

By Train

The two main Railway stations near Satlok Ashram are "Jind" and "Hisar". There is a satisfactory train service from Delhi (Old Delhi Railway Station and / or New Delhi Railway Station) to both "Jind" and "Hisar". There is also a train station in "Barwala".

Please visit the Indian Government Railway Website for information on train routes and timings.

By Air

The nearest international airport is Indira Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi. The airport is connected via "Bus", "Taxi" and "Metro" to ISBT (Inter State Bus Stand - Kashmere Gate) Delhi and Delhi Rail Station.

Shuttle Bus service Terminal 3 - ISBT

Delhi Transport Corporation (DTC) Shuttle Bus service is available outside the Arrivals Gate at Indira Gandhi International Airport Terminal 3. Buses run at a regular interval (every 30 minutes) connecting the airport to Connaught Place, New Delhi Railway Station & Interstate Bus Terminal (ISBT - Kashmere Gate).
